Located in the heart of Hyderabad, AJ Salon is the place where self-care meets real results.

Our Services

Everything you need to look great and feel confident — all in one place.

Haircut

A great cut is the foundation of any good look. Our stylists take the time to understand your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before picking up the scissors. Whether you want a bold new style or a clean, polished trim, you'll walk out with a cut that works for you.

Precision cut Style consultation included Men & women All hair types

Hair Spa

Restore shine, softness, and strength to dull or damaged hair with a nourishing hair spa treatment. Using deep-conditioning formulas, our therapists repair damage from heat, pollution, and chemical treatments — leaving your hair visibly healthier from the very first session.

Deep conditioning Damage repair Scalp nourishment Frizz control

Facial

Give your skin the attention it deserves. Our facials are customised to your skin type — whether oily, dry, or combination — and target common concerns like dullness, acne, uneven tone, and fine lines. Walk out with a refreshed, radiant glow that lasts for days.

Skin-type matched Deep cleansing Brightening & glow Anti-ageing options

Bridal Makeup

Your wedding day calls for a look that lasts as long as the memories. Our bridal makeup artists create stunning, camera-ready looks that complement your outfit, skin tone, and personal style — from classic traditional bridal to modern glam. Trial sessions available.

Trial session included Long-lasting formula Traditional & modern styles HD & airbrush finish

Manicure

Well-groomed hands make a statement. Our manicure treatments go beyond nail polish — with exfoliation, cuticle care, moisturising, and a professional finish, your hands will look polished and feel incredibly soft. Choose from classic, gel, or nail art options.

Cuticle care Classic & gel options Nail art available Long-lasting finish

Pedicure

Your feet carry you everywhere — it's time to treat them. Our pedicure services start with a relaxing soak, followed by thorough exfoliation, nail shaping, cuticle work, and a long-lasting polish of your choice. A truly indulgent experience from heel to toe.

Relaxing foot soak Full heel care Exfoliation & massage Chip-resistant polish
